MINI MCC

Ace Race

Ace Race is a racing game where players race against eachother on a course. The goal of the game is to complete three laps as fast as possible using power-ups and features such as Speed Boost pads, Launch Pads, and many more. Each Ace Race game takes 10-12 minutes.

Dodgebolt

Dodgebolt is the final game where the top two teams face off in a duel. Players use bows to eliminate opponents while dodging the other teams arrows. Each team starts off with one arrow, and the last team standing wins the event.

Grid Runners

Grid Runners is all about cooperation! Teams are catapulted into ten identical, parallel courses that contain challenge rooms that are perfectly designed to test players communication, Minecraft skills, and speed.

Survival Games

Survival Games (AKA Hunger Games) is a one-round PVP elimination game. Teams fight to become the last ones standing. Players must loot chests and supply drops for gear, weapons, and food. As the game progresses a world border forces players into the center of the map for an epic showdown.

Meltdown

Meltdown is a fast-paced team archery game! Freeze your opponents with arrows while using your heater to thaw teammates. Coin crates are scattered around rooms for bonus points, but watch out: the outer rooms periodically melt away until only one remains where survivors battle to the death.

Rocket Spleef Rush

Rocket Spleef Rush takes spleef to new heights, literally! Armed with an elytra and rocket launcher, players battle in the air and on floating islands while spleefing their opponents. When platforms are destroyed, it's a free for all to reach the next safe platform! Last team flying wins!

Railroad Rush

Railroad Rush is a race to build the best railway! Teams compete to construct the longest possible railway while managing their gold. Each rail costs gold, so managing your gold is important. Keep an eye out for bonus vaults scattered around that are stocked with both gold and coins!

TGTTOSAWAF

TGTTOSAWAF is a team based parkour game. Teams race to complete the course the fastest, and wack a NPC at the finnish line. Oh yeah, you can punch eachother off :D

Sky Battle

Survival Games but on floating islands!

Parkour Warrior

Parkour Warrior is all about movement! Players race through a course featuring a main path and 5 bonus paths. At the finish line, players choose between three endings: easy, medium, or hard. Harder endings provide bigger team multipliers. Snag extra coins by completing bonus paths along the way!
